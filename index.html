About $ALVIN

Many may ask, what is $ALVIN? Myro is the name of the dog owned by Raj Gokal, one of the co-founders of Solana. This project pays homage to him, and his dog, and responds to the popular demand for dog-based narratives in the crypto space. Myro was created in response to this demand, and to provide value to the ecosystem created by Raj and his partner — the Solana blockchain.

With Myro, we have created a revolutionary digital currency.
